Vision and Mission

Vision

“The realization of students as critical, creative and character-based learners who care about the environment”

Mission

  1. Creating a learning environment that supports the development of social, emotional, physical, and intellectual skills;
  2. Implementing student-centered learning;
  3. Provide opportunities for students to understand values ​​so that character can develop;
  4. Organizing programs that foster and develop a sense of environmental concern;
  5. Creating a school learning community;
  6. Creating active participation from all components of the school, including parents, in order to improve the quality of education;
  7. Continuously developing the quality of education in order to guarantee the quality of school education;
  8. Implementing learning that encourages students to think critically and creatively;
